About Me

I'm Matt Anderson, a self-taught developer with an interest in creating functional and beuatiful websites, and in learning more about the tools that we use to make them. My journey to programming is not traditional: I studied English in undergrad at the University of Michigan and then I taught high school English for a year. Although I don't have a CS degree, those previous occupations have helped shaped me into the person I am: highly curious, a fast learner, a good communicator, and a passionate team player. As I have learned to program in the past year, I have discovered a real passion for this sort of work: in the satisfaction of solving difficult problems and building useful tools, and in chance to always be learning something new.


In searching for a job as a web developer, I am looking for a position that allows me to constantly learn, explore satisfying solutions to complex problems, and to work with a team that supports my growth. If that sounds like a good fit for your organization, please use the form below to send me a quick message!
